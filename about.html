Watch About Donate Prizes

Penguins Marathons is a fundraising team based out of St.Catharines, Ontario. Since 2009 we have been running video game marathons in exclusive partnership with Big Brothers Big Sisters of St. Catharines, Thorold & District.

To date we have raised approximately $80,800 for Big Brothers Big Sisters of St. Catharines – Thorold & District through video game marathons. But we didn’t really raise that money, you did. Our previous marathons include: The Zelda Marathon ($2700), The Mystery Marathon ($3500), The Zelda Marathon 2 ($5000), The Mario Marathon ($2800), The Zelda Marathon 3 ($5500), The Metroid Marathon ($2700), The Zelda Marathon 4 ($10,000), The Zelda Marathon 5 ($16,000), The Zelda Marathon 6 ($14,000), The Zelda Marathon 7 ($11,500) and The Zelda Marathon 8 ($7100). For the past few years we have also been supporting Niagara Dog Rescue through the sale of t-shirts.


Our Partners

Big Brothers Big Sisters of Canada is a Federation comprised of 108 member agencies servicing more than 1,100 communities across the country. Together they mobilize over 21,300 volunteers who in turn mentor 41,700+ children and young people. We support their St. Catharines, Thorold & District agency.

Niagara Dog Rescue are a volunteer based, cross-border, registered not-for-profit organization. They are located in the Niagara Region, Ontario and focus on dogs at immediate risk at high kill shelters.
